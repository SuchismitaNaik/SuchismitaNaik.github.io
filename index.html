About

I am a final semester post-graduate student from National Institute of Design, R&D Bangalore campus. Currently, I am interning as information designer/data visualiser at Centre for Budget and Governance Accountability (CBGA), New Delhi.

Prior to this, I did my summer internship in the design team of Housing.com where I designed an application for real estate investors from its ideation phase till the final visual design. For my academic design project, I got an opportunity to work as system designer in TCS Innovation Labs which was in collaboration with MIT Media lab on solving the ‘Blood bank’ challenge in the ‘Health and hygiene’ theme. Recently, this project got selected as one of the top three projects to move into execution phase.

I am an engineer-turned-designer who loves to research, analyse, ideate and design an ideal solution to any problem. Though I love calculating things around me, but my inclination towards art, story-telling, aesthetics, uniformity and entirety is even more profound. So all in all, my brain is an assortment of numbers, design and of course Food.
